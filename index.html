Why Mounia Works

Check out the strand of hair below.

See how untreated hair looks like a pine cone? The rough surface of the untreated hair generates friction between hairs, reducing moisture and resulting in split ends, flyaways, and a dry flaky scalp. Typical serums and conditioners merely coat the cuticle in oil and silicone, which temporarily smooths the pine cone effect but creates a greasy look & feel as the product sloughs off throughout the day. Silicone & oil also paradoxically reduce the volume of the hair as they cause individual strands to stick together.

Mounia is specially formulated to not only smooth your damaged cuticles but to repair damaged keratin and protect your hair from further damage. We achieve this result through patented polymer technology.
